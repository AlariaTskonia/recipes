Ingredients:

 - 1 cup butter
 - half a medium onion, chopped
 - 2 cups rice
 - 4 cups water
 - 16 oz tomato sauce
 - 1/2 cup fresh, chopped cilantro
 - 1 tablespoon garlic powder
 - 2 teaspoons chili powder
 - 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon powder
 - salt to taste


Directions:
  1. Soften the chopped onions in the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add rice and brown.
  3. Pour rice and onion mixture into a large pot
  4. Stir in all remaining ingredients
  5. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer on low for 30 minutes.
